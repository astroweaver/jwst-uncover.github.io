Acknowledgement

Please cite the survey paper (Suess et al. 2024) for any publications using MegaScience data products, as well the other publications noted for specific releases.

As the MegaScience catalogs also include broad-band photometry from the UNCOVER survey (GO-2561; PIs: Bezanson & Labbe), please also cite the UNCOVER survey paper (Bezanson et al. 2022) in any publication using the UNCOVER+MegaScience data products.
